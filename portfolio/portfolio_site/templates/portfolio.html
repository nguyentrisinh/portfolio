GetMyInvoices is a cloud-based accounting and invoice management software designed primarily for freelancers and small business entrepreneurs. GetMyInvoices helps businesses fetch invoices from over 500 online portals, manage funds, and transfer invoice details to an integrated bookkeeping service such as Xero, Dropbox, FastBill, Zoho Books and Billy.
